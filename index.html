FAQ

How do I book an appointment with a doctor?
You can book an appointment online through our website by selecting your preferred doctor and available time slot. You may also call our helpdesk for assistance.
Do you accept health insurance?
Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card during your visit or contact us to confirm if your provider is accepted.
What should I bring for my first visit?
For your first visit, please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, any current medications, and prior medical records if available.
Are telehealth or online consultations available?
Yes, we offer telehealth services for certain conditions. You can schedule an online consultation through the “Virtual Visit” section of our website.
